Teaching experience

Approach

I bring the same breadth and curiosity that shapes my research into the classroom — drawing connections across disciplines and encouraging students to think critically about how people, places, and environments are entangled.

As a sessional instructor and teaching assistant at the University of Toronto, I am committed to creating inclusive, engaging learning environments. My teaching draws on my interdisciplinary background across the sciences and social sciences, supporting students to develop both analytical skills and an appreciation for the complexity of human-environment relationships.
